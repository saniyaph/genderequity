"Equal rights, equal might. Gender equity ignites, empowering all to reach new heights."

Gender equity refers to the principle of fairness and justice in the treatment of individuals, regardless of their gender, in all aspects of life. It aims to ensure that all people, regardless of their gender identity, have equal access to opportunities, resources, and benefits.

About Us

The focus for the Gender Equity Strategy 2018-2023 will be on the following six strategic areas:

1. Prevent and combat gender stereotypes and sexism

2. Prevent and combat violence against women and domestic violence

3. Ensure the equal access of women to justice

4. a balanced participation of women and men in political and public decision-making

5. Protect the rights of migrant, refugee and asylum-seeking women and girls

6. Achieve gender mainstreaming in all policies and measures
